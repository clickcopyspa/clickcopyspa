What is this?

ClickCopySpa is a simple single page application where you can store frequently copy and pasted information to easily find and copy it.

It was borne out of an SPA I made to help manage number plates for a digital visitor permit system, and I realized that it might be useful for any frequently copy and pasted information.

How do I use it?

Simply click the "Add" button on the main page to add data. The title and data are stored directly in your browser, which means you will only see them when you open this page with this browser on this device.

Once you have added an entry, click on row to copy it to your clipboard, ready for pasting wherever you need to.

You can also edit and delete your data

You can manually backup your data and restore it into another browser/device by clicking on the export button.

Is my data safe?

Yes. This system runs directly on GitHub using the code at https://github.com/clickcopyspa/clickcopyspa. All of your data is saved in your browser. We recommend exporting your data whenever you make a change and keeping it somewhere safe, just in case you lose access to the browser or device.

You can access this page from any browser on any device, but you will only see data that you have added (or restored) on that browser and device.

Why not just use notes?

Notes (or any other app where you can paste text) works just fine for this, but it's a bit more fiddly to copy and paste things, especially if they have spaces. You also have to find the right note, and then search for what you are looking for.

With this webapp, the things that you most frequently need to type are right there as soon as you open the page, and it's extremely easy to copy them by simply clicking on the data. It just makes the process easier, especially for those who struggle with copying and pasting text from their phone or tablet.
